# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for everyone involved in this project.

## Expected Behavior
- Use respectful and inclusive language
- Be constructive and professional
- Respect privacy, ethics, and applicable laws

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination
- Encouraging illegal or unethical OSINT usage
- Publishing private or sensitive data

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content or contributors that violate this code.

---

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, discussions, and pull requests.
